V1 Architecture

Transparent rubric, not hidden magic

This prototype uses explicit rule groups aligned to official guidance themes. It is not a validated epidemiologic model, and it should not be described as one.

Signal Severity

Driven by the targeted organism, whether the event is confirmed or preliminary, and whether there is evidence of linked transmission.

Spread Potential

Driven by population served, acute and long-term care footprint, transfers, and mixing across the local healthcare network.

Containment Fragility

Reflects lab access, screening capacity, isolation/disinfection readiness, and interfacility communication discipline.

Confidence & Caution

Missing local data does not silently inflate the evidence score. Instead, it lowers confidence and can elevate the recommended posture conservatively.
